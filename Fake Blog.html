Stephen Curry

Stephen Curry was born on March 14, 1988 in Akron, Ohio. He played college basketball at Davidson College from 2006-2009. During his college career, he was named the Southern Conference Player of the Year. In Curry's sophmore year in college, he set the record for most three pointers made in a single season. He was taken as the 7th overall pick in the NBA draft by the Golden State Warriors. Known as an underrated player; Curry soon became a household name winning two MVPs and three NBA Championships.
